Startup Description : Sonnet

Sonnet AI is a startup that aims to improve the sales and marketing industry by leveraging advances in Artificial Intelligence, specifically language modeling. Sonnet’s product automates writing personalized sales emails with relevant information tailored to individual prospects.

Sketching and Wireframing

Before we began sketching interfaces for our Sonnet application the team decided that a browser web application would be the most effective interface to start. The intended users for this application are sales representatives in a wide range of industries. For these users we wanted to provide a comfortable and familiar email experience to the email applications that they use in their daily life. With this in mind we designed the Sonnet interfaces to provide structure in the form of well structured directories, client tags, familiar email interfaces, etc to help facilitate their navigation throughout the application, while providing them the added value AI assistance to help them draft sales emails more quickly.

With the vision in mind, designed sketches with a focus on diversifying our layouts to generate more ideas. We generated several sketches each reimagning an interface for our application,in the end we had designs with the following themes:

  • Traditional Email Design
  • Embedded Plugin Design
  • Salesforce-like Design
  • Pinterest Board-like Design

User Testing

User testing is a crucial part of the iterative design process, it provides an avenue to validate assumptions and gauge experiences of actual users. We used the services of UserTesting.com to get real users to interact with our interactive solution to observe and improve based on their feedback We developed a multi-step task that took a user through a complete interaction cycle of Sonnet’s main functionality, while interacting with all the contact points of our high fidelity mockup. To clearly define the task to the user we presented instructions that provided context and highlighted the step by step subtask objectives.

Analysis of Results

Key Take Aways from Testing Results

  • Generally our test results were positive, with a significant number of user interaction choices and observations matching our design intuitions and conceptual model.
  • Testers found the interface easy to figure out and straight forward for accomplishing the intended functionality.
  • Two users expressed interest in a more sophisticated design while one user appreciated the current simplicity of the design. User demography revealed a correlation between the age of testers and their sentiments regarding sophistication of design.
  • Generally our testers commended our consistent color coding and the presented functionality, acknowledging its relevance for the Sales Industry. Our contact management feature was particularly appreciated.
